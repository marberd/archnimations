Revit & BIM

BIM drafting for buildings of all sizes.

The service covers industry-standard models, 3D modeling, 2D to 3D conversion and high-quality CD sets. We also help clients with construction scheduling and cost estimation. The goal is the maximum quality of service for each project's needs.

From model to documentation.

  • Industry-standard BIM models built to a consistent, coordinated standard.
  • 3D modeling of architectural elements and assemblies.
  • 2D to 3D conversion of existing CAD drawings into a live Revit model.
  • High-quality CD sets ready for permitting and construction.
  • Scheduling and cost estimation support for construction procedures.
Why Revit and BIM

BIM is the future of the construction industry.

Revit lets designers design, simulate, visualize and collaborate, capitalizing on the interconnected data inside a BIM model.

One coordinated model drives plans, sections, schedules and renders at once, so changes stay consistent and nothing falls out of sync.
