Revit & BIM

BIM drafting services for buildings of all sizes.

The service includes the creation of industry standards, 3D modeling, conversion from 2D to 3D, and CD sets of high quality. We also help clients with scheduling construction procedures and cost estimation. Our goal is to deliver the maximum quality of service, based on each project's needs and preferences.

What's included

From model to documentation.

  • Industry-standard BIM models built to a consistent, coordinated standard.
  • 3D modeling of architectural elements and assemblies.
  • 2D-to-3D conversion of existing CAD drawings into a live Revit model.
  • High-quality CD sets ready for permitting and construction.
  • Scheduling & cost estimation support for construction procedures.
Why Revit / BIM?

BIM is the future of the construction industry.

Revit helps designers design, simulate, visualize and collaborate — capitalizing on the advantages of the interconnected data within a BIM model.

One coordinated model drives plans, sections, schedules and renders at once, so changes stay consistent and nothing falls out of sync.
