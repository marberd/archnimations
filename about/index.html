About the studio

A design and technology studio for the built environment.

Archnimations uses 3D modeling software, AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, Lumion and Rhino, to produce detailed renderings, animation, architectural design and Revit consulting.

Tailored to your vision, no shortcuts.

Our design solutions are built around your project. We go the extra mile to understand the vision, and we do not cut corners on creativity or quality.

We are cost-effective and time-efficient, and our transparency is why clients keep coming back. We are in this business to do great work.

What we deliver.

  • Architectural rendering and animation. Realistic visualizations that sell the design.
  • 3D models. Presentation-ready models built from your plans and sketches.
  • Virtual tours and 360° panoramas. Immersive walkthroughs before construction.
  • Interior and exterior visualization. Residential, commercial and mixed-use.
  • Revit drafting and consulting. BIM documentation at industry standard.
