About the studio

We are passionate about all aspects of 3D visualization.

Archnimations is a design & technology-oriented studio. We use 3D modeling software — AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, Lumion and Rhino — to produce detailed renderings, animation, architectural design and Revit consulting.
